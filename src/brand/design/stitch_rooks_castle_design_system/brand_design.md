# Brand Design Guidelines: Rooks & Castle Identity

## Brand Essence
**Rooks & Castle** is an AI-powered business consultancy that balances cutting-edge technology with traditional professional values. The brand aesthetic is **editorial, calm, and confident**. It avoids "tech-hype" in favor of "strategic clarity."

---

## Typography
The system uses a high-contrast typographic pairing to evoke a premium, established feel.

### Display: Playfair Display
- **Usage:** Primary headlines and high-impact editorial statements.
- **Weights:** 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold).
- **Style:** Sentence case, tight letter-spacing (-0.03em).

### UI & Body: Manrope
- **Usage:** Secondary headlines, body prose, and all functional UI elements.
- **Weights:** 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 600 (SemiBold).
- **Style:** Highly readable, modern sans-serif.

### Accents: Montserrat
- **Usage:** Small labels, badges, and navigation items.
- **Style:** ALL CAPS, wide tracking (0.12em).

---

## Color Palette
A deep, immersive dark mode foundation with high-visibility functional accents and warm editorial secondary tones.

### Core Colors
- **Primary Background:** `#0F0F0F` (Deep Charcoal)
- **Surface/Cards:** `#1A1A1A` (Elevated Grey)
- **Borders:** `#2A2A2A` (Subtle Dividers)
- **Primary Accent:** `#10B981` (Emerald Green) — used for CTAs and highlights.
- **Accent Hover:** `#34D399` (Mint)

### Secondary & Editorial Accents (Orange/Amber)
- **Warm Amber:** `#F59E0B` — Used for secondary emphasis in imagery, data visualization, or subtle glows.
- **Amber Glow:** `rgba(245, 158, 11, 0.18)` — Used for background depth and soft editorial highlights.

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#F9FAFB` (Near-White)
- **Secondary Text:** `#9CA3AF` (Muted Grey)
- **Tertiary/Disabled:** `#6B7280` (Deep Grey)

---

## Visual Language

### Layout & Spacing
- **Container:** Max-width 1400px.
- **Grid:** 12-column layout with generous vertical whitespace (py-20 to py-32).
- **Sectioning:** Clean, horizontal dividers or subtle background shifts between sections.

### Components
- **Cards:** 16px to 24px border radius. Subtle 1px borders.
- **Glassmorphism:** Used sparingly for navigation or overlays (blur: 20px, opacity: 0.8).
- **Buttons:** 8px to 12px border radius. Primary buttons use the Emerald Green gradient.

### Imagery
- **Style:** Low-key, cinematic photography. Desaturated or monochromatic with green/amber light accents.
- **Abstract AI:** Symbolic representations (grids, nodes) rather than literal "robot" imagery.
- **Color Balance:** Balance the cool primary green with warm amber accents in hero photography to create a sophisticated, high-end editorial feel.

---

## Brand Principles
1. **Clarity over Spectacle:** Designs should be easy to navigate and understand.
2. **Immersive Depth:** Use layered surfaces and subtle glows (green and amber) to create a 3D feel on a 2D screen.
3. **Intentionality:** Every element should serve a purpose; if it doesn't add value, remove it.
